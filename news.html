Press Release • 18 September 2023

Tenanting Set to Begin at Goodwood Mega Social Housing Project

Tenanting is officially set to begin in November 2023 at the City-enabled Goodwood social housing mega-project. This landmark complex will provide rental housing to more than 1,000 residents and their families.

Cape Town Mayor Geordin Hill-Lewis inspected the advanced construction progress on Monday 18 September, accompanied by the Mayoral Committee Member for Human Settlements, Councillor Carl Pophaim. During the inspection, municipal leaders highlighted the critical role of dense, central social housing in restructuring urban layouts.

By integrating affordable apartments directly onto major public transport loops, the Goodwood Station mega-project allows qualifying tenants earning between R1,800 and R22,500 to easily access commuter rails and city business sectors.

Announcements • 22 January 2024

Prasa on Track to Provide Accommodation to 3,200 NSFAS Students in Cape Town

The Passenger Rail Agency of South Africa (PRASA) has partnered with a regional property developer to lease and manage state-of-the-art accommodation for more than 3,200 students.

Students eligible for National Student Financial Aid Scheme (NSFAS) funding will be eligible to relocate directly into the newly constructed residential complex near Cape Town Station in February 2024.

This massive development combines smart city initiatives, sustainable energy integrations, and safe, affordable housing structures. Proximity to primary commuter hubs ensures that students face zero-friction transits to universities across the Cape Town metropolitan area.

Public Reviews • 19 September 2023

Mayor Geordin Hill-Lewis Confirms Goodwood Project Success

Following the recent inspection of the Goodwood Station mega-project, Cape Town Mayor Geordin Hill-Lewis confirmed rapid structural progress.

"Our big social housing project at Goodwood Station is progressing well. It will provide affordable rental accommodation for 1000 families, such an important life-improving opportunity for many."

FCI Community Housing Services continues to supervise on-site caretakers, manage application intakes, and coordinate with municipal developers to ensure immediate tenancies proceed seamlessly.
