Flagship Mega-Project

Goodwood Station Development

Bridging transport access and modern community living for over 1,000 families.

FCI Community Housing Services secured the structural development rights awarded by PRASA (Passenger Rail Agency of South Africa) for high-density, mixed-use transit-oriented complexes around Goodwood and Heideveld stations.

RCG approval by the Social Housing Regulatory Authority (SHRA) was officially reached in December 2017. Construction was completed, and tenanting officially initiated in late 2023. By offering high-quality living accommodations within immediate walking distance of commuter rail lines, we return low-to-medium income families directly to transport networks, modern schools, and primary workspaces.

"This mega-project stands as a premier example of City-enabled spatial restructuring, delivering life-improving opportunities and high-standard affordable rentals directly on transport nodes."
