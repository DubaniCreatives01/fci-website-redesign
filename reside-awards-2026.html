Gold Award: Social Housing Project of the Year

FCI Community Housing Services has been awarded the highly prestigious **Reside Awards 2026 Gold Award** for the landmark Goodwood Station Social Housing Development.

The Reside Awards are widely recognized as the premier property and social housing developer recognition program in South Africa, celebrating exceptional achievements in community-centric property developments, architectural innovation, and social upliftment.

Goodwood Station Social Housing was selected by an independent panel of industry experts, municipal planners, and academic leads as the outstanding **Social Housing Project of the Year**. The development was recognized for setting a benchmark in South African urban planning, demonstrating that affordable housing can be synonymous with visual excellence, energy efficiency, and modern community design.

Key Pillars of Excellence

  • ✓
    Transit-Oriented Integration: Positioned directly adjacent to primary commuter rail and bus networks, reconnecting families with economic corridors and urban social amenities.
  • ✓
    Premium Living Spaces: Providing Bachelor, 1-Bedroom, and 2-Bedroom layouts styled with clean aesthetics, durable modern finishes, and rich community courtyards.
  • ✓
    Sustainability Accreditations: Equipped with energy-efficient heat pumps, water conservation technologies, and natural light optimization to dramatically reduce carbon footprints and monthly utility costs.
