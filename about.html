Our Roots

Gearing For Sustainable Social Restructuring

FCI Community Housing Services t/a FCI CHS is a registered Section 21 non-profit organization established in July 2012.

Our institution was specifically created to act as a robust developmental and management vehicle to access state housing subsidies for the creation of rental and gap housing in the Western Cape and across South Africa.

We develop premium-quality, well-located affordable housing accommodation close to primary employment hubs, mass transport corridors, quality schools, and essential social infrastructure. Our core goal is to bridge the massive housing gap for low-to-medium income families who are excluded from the formal private credit and housing markets, specifically including:

  • ✓ Families with special needs, particularly disabled citizens
  • ✓ Young adults and single earners with a steady formal income
  • ✓ Single parent households facing urban exclusion

Our Vision

To become a leading, state-accredited Social Housing Institution, consistently developing and managing superior-quality, highly affordable social, rental, and gap housing complexes on a self-sufficient and financially sustainable basis.

Our Mission

To build an organization capable of directly contributing toward the spatial restructuring of South African cities by providing well-located, affordable living units to the low-to-middle income earners excluded from traditional mortgage credit.

Strategic Goals

  • • Establish public/private restructuring partnerships.
  • • Scale housing production in peri-urban markets.
  • • Supplement capacity of matching non-profit entities.
  • • Ensure absolute corporate self-sufficiency.

Guiding Principles

Our corporate rules govern our operational framework, project conceptualization, and management choices:

01

Delivering Value & Affordability

Consistently demonstrating why we are the first choice for quality, service, value, and structural innovation while ensuring rents remain affordable.

02

Operational Sustainability

Maintaining highly efficient and cost-effective management across all complexes to guarantee the long-term viability of social housing assets.

03

Integrity, Transparency & Governance

Ensuring honest, principled, and fully auditable operations under deep accountability to our clients, regulatory stakeholders, and communities.

Institutional Credentials

SHRA Accredited Housing Institution +
Conditionally accredited by the Social Housing Regulatory Authority (SHRA) under the Social Housing Act, enabling us to acquire and manage government-subsidized rental stock.
CIDB & NHBRC Registered Builder +
Registered with the Construction Industry Development Board (CIDB) and NHBRC as a Home Builder, confirming our adherence to national building and engineering regulations.
100% Black Owned - BEE Level 1 +
FCI is proudly a 100% Black-owned organization and a certified Level 1 B-BBEE contributor, driving meaningful socio-economic development and structural job creation.
