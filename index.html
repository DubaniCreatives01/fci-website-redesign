Transforming Urban Landscapes, Empowering Lives

FCI Community Housing Services was established in 2012 to develop and manage quality, affordable social rental housing across South Africa.

Particularly focused on peri-urban and rural areas battling with severe housing backlogs, we act as a highly accredited, specialized vehicle leveraging public and private partnerships to access government subsidies. Our developments reconnect low-to-medium income earners with transport nodes, economic opportunities, and social amenities.

Our Core Specializations

Providing fully integrated housing solutions, operational property management, and sustainable gap-market developments.

Affordable Rental Housing

Developing and owning high-density, beautifully designed, and sustainable rental units for households earning between R1,800 and R22,000.

Gap Housing Accommodation

Providing direct rental opportunities for citizens who fall within the gap market, ineligible for BNG subsidies yet excluded from conventional bank funding.

Flagship Re-development

Goodwood Station Social Housing Project

Providing rentals to more than 1,000 low-to-medium income families.

Partnering with PRASA and the City of Cape Town, FCI secured exclusive development rights around Goodwood Station to construct a massive mixed-use transit-oriented community hub. This project enables residents to live comfortably close to rail corridors, social schools, and workplaces.
