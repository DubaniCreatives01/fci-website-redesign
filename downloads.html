Qualifying Rules

Tenant Application Guidelines

Thank you for your interest in FCI Community Housing Services. To apply for our active social housing projects (including Goodwood Station), please review the basic qualifying criteria:

  • 1
    Joint Household Income: Applicant joint household income must fall strictly between R1,800 and R22,500 per month (subject to specific unit caps).
  • 2
    Citizenship & Status: Must be a South African citizen or have permanent residency status, with clean credit histories.
  • 3
    Subsidy Status: Must not have previously owned residential property or accessed government housing subsidy programs.

Required Supporting Documents

Please attach certified copies of the following when submitting your form:

  • • Certified South African ID Copy (for all household members)
  • • 3 Months Recent Bank Statements
  • • 3 Months Recent Payslips (or proof of income)
  • • Certified Marriage Certificate / Divorced Decree (if applicable)
  • • Proof of Current Residential Address
